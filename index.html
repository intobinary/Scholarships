Frequently Asked Questions

  • 01.
    Who qualifies for a scholarship?

    Our citeria for selections include academic excellence, characteristics of leadership, courage, determination, and the ability to influence their community positively.

  • 02.
    To what extent do you help the learner?

    Applicants must qualify for a 100% school fee exemption in terms of the GDE regulations. In 2021, this equated to a maximum gross family income below R372 000 per annum.

  • 03.
    Where do I apply?

    Applicants must be nominated by their school principal or campus dean. The deadline for nominations is always at the start of each project.
